# Product Roadmap

## Vision

Make every playable court-hour easy to discover, fill, and enjoy at a fair value for both players and venues.

## Strategy

Win dense local markets by combining trustworthy live inventory, effortless group coordination, and transparent off-peak recommendations. Learn from actual schedule flexibility and booking behavior while protecting venue margin and user trust.

The product will not begin with unrestricted AI pricing, nationwide expansion, or a broad social network. It will first establish booking reliability and liquidity in selected venue clusters.

## Capabilities

### Marketplace foundation

- Venue and court onboarding
- Live inventory calendar
- Location/time/price search
- Booking, payment, cancellation, and refund

### Personalized discovery

- Preference onboarding
- Best-value adjacent time and venue alternatives
- Rebooking shortcuts
- Explainable recommendations

### Demand and group formation

- Invitations and split payment
- Open games
- Skill and reliability profiles
- Recurring groups

### Inventory and yield tools

- Owner promotion controls
- Waitlist and cancellation recovery
- Occupancy heatmaps
- Demand forecast and campaign recommendations

### Trust and lifecycle

- Verified venues and reviews
- Notification preferences
- Loyalty and referral
- Privacy and data controls

## Success Metrics

### North star

- Incremental occupied court-hours per active venue per week

### Player outcomes

- Median time to selected slot below 60 seconds
- Search-to-book conversion
- 30-day repeat booking rate
- Percentage of bookings using group tools
- Recommendation acceptance and personalization lift

### Venue outcomes

- Off-peak occupancy lift
- Revenue per available court-hour
- Cancellation refill rate
- Active inventory accuracy
- Venue retention

### Guardrails

- Realized price and contribution margin
- No-show and cancellation rate
- Refund completion time
- Notification opt-out
- Fairness/privacy complaints

## Time Horizon

### Now: Validate and transact

- Interview players and venue owners
- Launch one dense city zone with 5-10 venues
- Build inventory, search, booking, payment, and owner calendar
- Add browse-first preference onboarding
- Add transparent, rules-based off-peak offers
- Instrument all discovery and booking events

### Next: Create and recover demand

- Waitlists and cancellation refill
- Invite, split payment, and recurring group
- Open games and basic skill matching
- Owner occupancy dashboard and campaign suggestions
- Controlled experiments with generic holdout experiences
- Student/corporate programs with explicit eligibility

### Later: Optimize the network

- Predictive slot demand and no-show risk
- Contextual recommendation and notification timing
- Multi-venue passes and memberships
- League and tournament tools
- Conversational booking
- Multi-city expansion after local liquidity thresholds are met
