# Journey Map: LMS Career Pathway Builder

## Discover
Alex logs into their daily LMS portal to check a chemistry grade. They notice a new, prominent banner on the dashboard: *"Unsure about college? Let AI map your future based on your actual strengths. Try the Pathway Builder."*

## Aware
Alex clicks the banner and is taken to a sleek, modern landing page within the LMS. They see that the tool uses their past 3 years of grades and cross-references them with live job market data. They are intrigued because it promises to be specific to *them*, not a generic quiz.

## Convert
Alex clicks "Start My Map." They spend 3 minutes swiping left/right on various tasks and interests (Tinder-style interface for low friction). They explicitly consent to let the AI analyze their LMS grade history and submitted essays. The system generates their personalized "Top 3 Career Pathways."

## Retain (Retent)
The recommendations aren't static. Two months later, Alex gets an 'A' in a computer science elective. The LMS sends a contextual nudge: *"Your recent success in CS has boosted your match score for 'UX Engineering'. Click here to see the updated market outlook for this career."* Alex regularly returns to the dashboard to adjust their saved interests and see how their pathway shifts.

## Advocate
Alex shows the "Market Opportunity" chart for UX Engineering to their parents, successfully convincing them it is a viable major. Alex tells their friends in homeroom, "You have to try that new tab on the LMS, it actually suggested something cool instead of just telling me to be a lawyer."
