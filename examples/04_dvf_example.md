# DVF Framework: LMS Career Pathway Builder

## Desirable (for users)
**Yes.** User research indicates that over 75% of high school juniors experience moderate to severe anxiety regarding college major selection. Existing solutions are generic 100-question surveys taken once a year. Students deeply desire real-time, hyper-personalized guidance that translates their current daily effort (grades) into future tangible outcomes (careers).

## Viable (for businesses)
**Yes.** For our LMS company, school districts are our buyers. Districts are evaluated (and often funded) based on student success metrics, college acceptance rates, and graduation rates. Offering this as a "Premium Tier" add-on increases our Average Revenue Per User (ARPU) and creates a strong competitive moat against other standard LMS platforms that only offer grade tracking.

## Feasible (for engineers)
**Yes.** 
*   **Data:** We already possess the proprietary historical grade and assignment data within our LMS. 
*   **AI/Matching:** We can utilize open-source LLMs to perform semantic matching between a student's profile and job descriptions.
*   **Market Data:** We don't need to build economic forecasts; we can integrate via API with established labor market data providers (e.g., Lightcast, Burning Glass, or Bureau of Labor Statistics) to pull real-time projected growth and salary data.
