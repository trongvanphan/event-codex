# Pitch Deck Outline: LMS Career Pathway Builder

## Presenting
**Presenter:** Lead Product Manager for LMS Expansion.
**The Hook:** "Right now, 1 in 3 college students will change their major at least once, costing them an extra semester of tuition and delaying their entry into the workforce. Why? Because we are asking 16-year-olds to guess their future without giving them any data."

## Problem Statement
High school students suffer from extreme anxiety regarding college choices. They receive minimal, generic counseling. They don't understand how their current daily academic effort translates to the future, leading to disengagement and poor college major selection.

## Solutions
**The LMS Career Pathway Builder:** An AI-driven engine integrated directly into the tool students already use every day. It continuously maps their historical grades and self-reported interests to real-world careers, recommending suitable college majors backed by live labor market data.

## Desirability
User research shows 80% of juniors are stressed about college. They reject generic quizzes but crave hyper-personalized, data-backed guidance. They want the "Spotify Discover Weekly" experience, but for their future careers.

## Viability
School districts are under immense pressure from parents and state boards to improve "College and Career Readiness" metrics. By offering this as a Premium Tier add-on, we can increase our Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) by 15% across our existing client base, creating a sticky feature that our competitors lack.

## Feasibility
We already own the hardest piece of the puzzle: the student's historical academic data. By combining this proprietary data with off-the-shelf Labor Market APIs (like Lightcast) and standard semantic matching algorithms, we can deliver a high-value MVP within 4 months without needing to invent new underlying AI architectures.
