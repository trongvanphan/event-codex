# Product Roadmap: LMS Career Pathway Builder

## Vision
Every student confidently steps into a future tailored to their unique potential, effectively bridging the gap between classroom learning and the future economy.

## Strategy
Transform our LMS from a passive "record-keeping" tool into a proactive, AI-driven "career navigation" engine. We will win by leveraging the data we already hold (grades/activity) to provide personalization that third-party career websites simply cannot access.

## Capabilities
*   **Theme 1: Data Ingestion & Assessment:** A frictionless, swipe-based interest assessment tool that merges with historical LMS grade data.
*   **Theme 2: The Recommendation Engine:** AI semantic matching algorithm connecting student profiles to a database of 500+ college majors and careers.
*   **Theme 3: Market Intelligence:** Integration of external APIs to display salary, projected 10-year job growth, and automation risk for recommended careers.
*   **Theme 4: Counselor/Parent Portal:** A dashboard for counselors to view student pathways and intervene effectively.

## Success Metrics
*   **HEART Metrics:**
    *   **Happiness:** Increase in student Net Promoter Score (NPS) for the LMS platform.
    *   **Engagement:** Percentage of eligible students (Juniors/Seniors) who complete the initial assessment.
    *   **Retention:** Weekly Active Users (WAU) returning to the Pathway Dashboard to explore.
    *   **Task Success:** Percentage of students who "Save/Pin" at least 3 target majors to their profile by the end of Q2.

## Time Horizon
*   **Q1 (Now):** Swipe-based Interest Assessment & Basic Algorithm Mapping.
*   **Q2 (Next):** Student Dashboard UI & Labor Market API Integration (Salary/Growth charts).
*   **Q3 (Later):** Contextual Nudges (e.g., LMS alerts suggesting courses based on chosen career path).
*   **Q4 (Later):** Parent & Counselor reporting dashboards.
