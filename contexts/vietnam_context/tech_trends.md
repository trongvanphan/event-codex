# Vietnam Context: Tech Trends & Digital Transformation

## The AI Revolution & Mainstream Adoption
Vietnam's technology landscape in 2024–2025 is defined by a rapid, widespread integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI).
*   **Rapid Growth:** AI adoption among Vietnamese businesses surged by approximately 39% year-on-year in 2025. By the end of 2025, nearly 170,000 enterprises were implementing AI solutions.
*   **Generative AI (GenAI):** GenAI has become a primary driver of operational efficiency. Approximately 61% of businesses using AI reported revenue growth, while 58% cited significant cost savings.
*   **Consumer Engagement:** As of 2025, 78% of Vietnam’s online population has engaged with AI platforms, heavily driven by Gen Z.

## Comprehensive Digital Transformation
*   **Government-Led Momentum:** Digital transformation is a national strategic priority under "Make in Vietnam" initiatives. The goal is to make Vietnam a top digital economy in ASEAN by 2030, with the digital economy accounting for at least **30% of GDP**.
*   **Economic Impact:** The digital economy is the fastest-growing in Southeast Asia, with an annual growth rate exceeding 20%. Revenue from e-commerce reached an estimated 36 billion USD in 2025, a threefold increase from 2020.
*   **Digital Infrastructure:** Internet penetration stands at nearly 79%, with over 110 million mobile broadband subscriptions and world-leading telecommunications infrastructure ranking 11th globally for broadband speeds.

## Key Shifts
*   **Digital Companionship:** The rise of conversational AI and virtual assistants to address social needs.
*   **Social & Chat Commerce:** Heavy utilization of messaging apps (Zalo, Facebook Messenger) for B2C interactions.
*   **Digital Payments:** Universal adoption of subscription models and digital wallets (MoMo, ZaloPay, VNPay).
